OEM & ODM Custom Manufacturing

From concept to container — we create outdoor camping mats and sleeping pads tailored to your brand, market, and specifications.

Our Customization Process

A proven 4-step workflow that turns your product vision into reality — transparent, efficient, and reliable.

1

Consultation & Design

Share your concept, reference product, or sketch. We discuss materials, construction, cost targets, and feasibility — free consultation.

2

Sampling & Iteration

We produce pre-production samples in 7-15 days. Iterate colors, textures, sizes, and details until perfect. Sample cost refundable on bulk orders.

3

Mass Production

Once the sample is approved, production begins with strict AQL 2.5 quality control at every stage. Regular progress updates with photos.

4

Inspection & Delivery

Pre-shipment inspection, professional packaging, container loading, and global logistics — door-to-door service available.

What Can Be Customized

Nearly every aspect of your camping mat can be tailored to your brand and target market.

📐

Dimensions & Thickness

Custom length, width, and thickness — single, double, family sizes. Thickness from 2.5cm ultralight to 10cm luxury comfort.

🎨

Colors & Patterns

Any Pantone color for fabric — solid colors, two-tone, camouflage, custom prints. Surface patterns: diamond, wave, egg trough, snowflake, peanut, custom texture.

🧵

Materials

PVC, TPU, nylon composite, polyester taffeta, suede, brushed fabric, pongee — choose based on weight, durability, comfort, and budget requirements.

🏷️

Branding & Private Label

Custom logo printing (silk-screen, heat transfer, embossed), custom labels, hang tags, and branded packaging — build your own outdoor brand.

📦

Packaging

Custom stuff sacks, carry bags, retail boxes, hang-sell packaging, color boxes — designed for your sales channel (online retail, big box, specialty outdoor stores).

⚙️

Features & Accessories

Built-in pillow, fast valve, insulation layer, anti-slip base, repair kit, connection system (double mat joining), R-Value optimization.

🔬

Compliance & Testing

Custom testing for target markets — REACH, RoHS, Prop 65, ASTM, EN 71. Test reports provided for your compliance documentation.

💰

Pricing Tiers

Flexible pricing based on materials and order volume. Entry-level PVC mats to premium TPU — we match your target retail price point.

OEM vs ODM: What's Right for You?

OEM Manufacturing

You provide the design. We manufacture to your exact specifications — perfect for established brands with existing product lines.

✓ Your design, our production
✓ Full IP and design protection (NDA)
✓ Mold and tooling managed by you
✓ Exact specification replication

ODM Manufacturing

We design and manufacture. Start with our proven designs and customize — ideal for startups and brands expanding into camping gear.

✓ Proven product base, customized for you
✓ Faster time-to-market
✓ Lower development cost
✓ Our expertise in materials and construction
