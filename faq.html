Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from B2B buyers about our products, services, and ordering process.

Are you a manufacturer or trading company?

Xiaoqin Outdoor (Yiwu Xiaoqin Electronic Commerce Co., Ltd.) is a sourcing and export company with deep partnerships with 50+ verified factories in Zhejiang Province. We are not a single factory — we are your sourcing partner who selects and manages the best factory for your specific product requirements. This model gives you access to multiple specialized factories, competitive pricing, and flexible production capacity that a single factory cannot match.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our typical MOQ is 100-300 units per SKU (depending on product type), but we welcome trial orders as low as 50 units for new buyers. For custom/OEM orders, the MOQ may be higher (500-1,000 units) due to material and setup costs. We're flexible and work with businesses at all stages — from startups to established brands.

Can I customize colors, sizes, and materials?

Yes, nearly everything can be customized. This includes: colors (any Pantone match), dimensions (custom length × width × thickness), materials (PVC, TPU, nylon composite, suede, brushed fabrics), surface patterns, valve types, built-in pillows, packaging, branding, and labels. For OEM projects, we can also develop entirely new designs from your sketches or reference products.

How long does it take to get samples?

For existing products, stock samples ship within 3-5 business days. For custom/OEM samples, production takes 7-15 days depending on complexity. We ship via DHL, FedEx, UPS, or SF Express — delivery to most countries takes 3-7 days. Sample cost is refundable when you place a bulk order.

What is your typical production lead time?

Standard production lead time is 25-35 days for orders of 1,000-5,000 units. Larger orders may require 35-50 days. Rush production is available for an additional fee. Lead times vary by product complexity, material availability, and factory schedule — we provide accurate timelines during quotation.

What payment terms do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C (letter of credit), PayPal, and Western Union. Standard terms: 30% deposit to start production, 70% balance before shipment (or against B/L copy for established buyers). For long-term partners, we offer more flexible payment arrangements.

How do you ensure product quality?

We follow AQL 2.5 quality standards with inspection at four stages: incoming materials, in-process production, final inspection, and pre-shipment random sampling. We personally oversee quality at partner factories and provide detailed inspection reports with every order. Third-party inspection (SGS, TÜV, Bureau Veritas) is available upon request.

Do you offer private labeling and custom packaging?

Yes. We support full private labeling including custom logo printing (silk-screen, heat transfer, embossing), custom labels, hang tags, and branded packaging — from simple stuff sacks to retail-ready color boxes. Our design team can help create packaging that meets your market's requirements.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are compliant with major international standards. Available certifications and test reports include: REACH (EU chemical compliance), RoHS, California Prop 65, EN 71, ASTM, and BSCI factory audit reports. We can arrange additional testing for specific market requirements — just let us know what you need.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We ship by sea (FCL/LCL), air freight, and express courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS). Sea freight is the most cost-effective for bulk orders — we can quote CIF, FOB, or DDP terms. Our logistics team handles container booking, customs documentation, and coordinates with your freight forwarder if preferred.

Can you source products beyond camping mats?

Yes. Through our 50+ factory network, we can source tents, sleeping bags, hammocks, camping furniture, outdoor cooking equipment, dry bags, and virtually any outdoor gear. Think of us as your one-stop outdoor product sourcing agent in Zhejiang — one contact for all your outdoor procurement needs.

How do you protect my product designs?

We sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before discussing any proprietary designs. All partner factories involved in your project also sign confidentiality agreements. We take intellectual property protection seriously and never share your designs with other clients without explicit permission.

Do I need special certifications to import camping mats?

For most common camping mats (PVC, TPU, foam), no special import license is required in most countries. However, you should ensure products meet your market's chemical and safety standards: REACH for EU, Prop 65 for California, and general product safety regulations for your region. We can provide test reports and compliance documentation to support your customs clearance.

What is your warranty and after-sales policy?

We stand behind our products. Manufacturing defects are covered — we provide replacement units or credit for defective items discovered upon receipt. We require photographic evidence and will resolve claims within 5 business days. For recurring quality issues, we investigate the root cause with the factory and implement corrective actions.

Still Have Questions?

Contact us directly — we typically respond within 12 hours on business days.
