Quality Control System

We follow AQL 2.5 standards for every order — from raw material inspection to pre-shipment final check.

QC Stage Inspection Scope Standard Frequency
Incoming Material Fabric weight, TPU/PVC film thickness, foam density, color fastness Factory specification sheet Every batch
In-Process Welding strength, seam integrity, valve function, dimensional accuracy AQL 2.5 Level II Every 2 hours during production
Final Inspection Visual defects, inflation test, packaging, labeling, accessories AQL 2.5 Level II 100% of finished goods
Pre-Shipment Random sampling, carton drop test, quantity verification AQL 2.5 Level I Before each shipment
✓ Third-party inspection available upon request (SGS, TÜV, Bureau Veritas)
✓ Detailed Inspection Reports provided with every shipment
✓ Defect classification: Critical / Major / Minor
✓ Factory audit reports available for serious buyers
